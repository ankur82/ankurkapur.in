A framework built around
your life stage,
not the market cycle.

Every advisory engagement is organised around a four-stage life framework. Your financial plan looks very different depending on where you are. The framework ensures the advice is always calibrated to that reality — not a generic template applied to everyone.

I
Accumulation
Building the base
Early career to peak earning years. The focus is on building wealth systematically — saving rate, asset allocation, compounding, and avoiding expensive mistakes.
II
Transition
The inflection point
Career changes, business exits, liquidity events, or major life shifts. High-stakes decisions that require clear thinking and careful structuring.
III
Distribution
Living from what you built
Retirement and beyond. The shift from accumulation to sustainable withdrawal — making the corpus last, managing tax, and preserving optionality.
IV
Legacy
What you leave behind
Succession, estate planning, philanthropy, and intergenerational wealth transfer. Ensuring the work of a lifetime is passed on with intention.
